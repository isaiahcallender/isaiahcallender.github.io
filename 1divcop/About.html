About

Welcome to Linda’s, where tradition meets modern Tex-Mex flair!
Originally a humble tortillería, this family-owned gem has transformed under the
care of second generation owner, Linda. She grew up with Mexican heritage and a nod
to the vibrant Oaxacan culture of her family’s original restaurant.

Linda is reimagining this restaurant in Chicago to capture the spirit of both past & present.
Whether you're a working professional, young family, or a long-time local,
Linda’s invites you to enjoy a fresh, authentic dining experience
that’s as colorful as her own childhood memories.
